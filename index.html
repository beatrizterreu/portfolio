About me

Design with purpose,
impact with data

I'm a Design Lead with over 15 years leading digital design teams and projects in international environments. I specialise in turning complex problems into simple, elegant and effective experiences, combining strategic vision with precise execution.

My multidisciplinary background has taken me across industries as diverse as fashion, food, sport, technology, insurance and weddings — always keeping the user at the centre of every decision.

I currently lead design at The Knot Worldwide, where I was recognised with the Core Value Award 2023 in the "We Love Our Users" category. I also train emerging designers in UX methodologies, digital ethics and accessibility.
